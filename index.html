Description

Strung Along is a cooperative puzzle-platformer that features a pair of marionettes working together to escape a cursed stage show. Players must work together, making use of their strings in order to get from point A to B and progress through the play. The game is broken into three acts which feature a focus on different mechanics and aesthetics to split up the gameplay. These acts are further broken down into scenes (which function as levels) with each act currently featuring three scenes that the players must work together to complete. The game is ideal for friends, couples, and acquaintances alike as it aims to bring people together through cooperative and communicative elements. Additionally, Strung Along is family friendly and can be played by anyone of all ages and genders.
